Visit Squareknot

Squareknot has the most beautiful, doable guides in the world, so you can do what you love without anything getting in your way. Like flow charts, guides on Squareknot can have multiple endings, so they can be adjusted to your own personal tastes and preferences. Follow the path you love most; they're all added by Squareknot's community of makers. So whether you're doing something familiar, or discovering something totally unexpected, there will always be an endless amount of awesome things to do that you love on Squareknot.

Squareknot was founded in early 2013 as a pivot from GoodSemester and grew to become one of the most notable startups in the Philadelphia region, raising more than $1.3 million from all-local investors and helping to kickstart the StartupPHL Angel fund with Josh Kopelman of First Round Capital.
