Visit Zelda Wiki

Zelda Wiki was originally founded in 2005 by myself and Zelda Universe forum member Jon Partridge (aka "Yami") as the Zelda Universe Wiki out of pure boredom. The project quickly outgrew its roots and was renamed Zelda Wiki, given its own domain, and other Zelda fan communities were invited to help build the wiki with us. This experiment in cross-community collaboration was an incredible success, and since then Zelda Wiki has grown many times the size of Zelda Universe, co-founding the NIWA wiki network and becoming the world's definitive source of information on Nintendo's Legend of Zelda franchise.

In 2017, Zelda Wiki and its team was acquired by Twitch and became a part of Gamepedia.
