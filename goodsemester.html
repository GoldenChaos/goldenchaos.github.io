An online learning platform ahead of its time.

I created GoodSemester in 2009 as a truly modern learning management system for colleges and universities. It contained a full suite of tools for online education including an interactive grade book, collaborative note taking tools, discussion boards, group work, assignment submission and more.

GoodSemester entered public beta in early 2012 after a lengthy private beta with multiple universities. However, after we discovered that users in the general public primarily wanted tutorial creation tools, the service was shut down and the company pivoted into Squareknot.
